Our Vision

A borderless financial ecosystem for 1.4 billion

AmidTech LLC is a US-registered technology company founded by entrepreneur Mahmoud Sagdoun, developing next-generation fintech platforms engineered for Africa's unique financial landscape.

Operating at the intersection of technology, compliance, and humanitarian impact — we build infrastructure that works where traditional banking cannot reach.

Incorporated in New Mexico, USA, with active operations in Niamey, Republic of Niger, AmidTech brings American legal credibility and African terrain intelligence together.

Pan-African Remittance Platform
