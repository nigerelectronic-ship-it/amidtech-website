AmidTech LLC is a US-registered technology company founded by entrepreneur Mahmoud Sagdoun, developing next-generation fintech platforms engineered for Africa's unique financial landscape.

Operating at the intersection of technology, compliance, and humanitarian impact — we build infrastructure that works where traditional banking cannot reach: from urban fintech hubs to rural Saharan communities via Starlink connectivity.

Incorporated in New Mexico, USA, with active operations in Niamey, Republic of Niger, AmidTech brings American legal credibility and African terrain intelligence together in one company.

Our Commitment

Security is not a feature.
It's our foundation.

At AmidTech, we recognize that trust is the most critical currency in financial services. Every system, API, and user interaction is designed with security as the primary constraint — not an afterthought.

Our security architecture follows NIST Cybersecurity Framework guidelines and undergoes continuous penetration testing. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256, and our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers.
